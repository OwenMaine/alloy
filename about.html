Welcome to Alloy Welding

As a national premier fabricator of waste disposal containers, Alloy Welding & Manufacturing has established itself as a trusted partner for industrial, multi-dwelling housing, and recycling facilities across the country.

From our state-of-the-art facility in St Paul, Minnesota, we specialize in manufacturing a comprehensive range of containers including:

  • Front Load Containers
  • Rear Load Containers
  • Roll Off Containers
  • Recycling Containers
  • Luggers
  • Rotator Boxes
